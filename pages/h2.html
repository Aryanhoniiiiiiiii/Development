HOW WE STARTED

Nova Times began with a simple yet profound passion for timeless craftsmanship and vintage elegance. Our founders, lifelong enthusiasts of classic watchmaking, envisioned a brand that celebrates the artistry and precision of traditional timepieces while catering to modern tastes. Starting as a small boutique dedicated to sourcing rare, vintage-inspired designs, we grew through an unwavering commitment to quality and a deep appreciation for the stories behind each watch. Today, Nova Times stands as a testament to that vision, offering a collection that blends heritage with luxury, bringing our love for exceptional timekeeping to your wrist.
